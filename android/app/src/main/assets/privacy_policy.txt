KOVA Parental Control - Privacy Policy

KOVA is a parental control and child safety application designed to help parents monitor and protect their children's digital activities. This privacy policy explains what data KOVA collects and how it is used.

What KOVA Monitors:
- Text messages and conversations in messaging apps (for safety analysis)
- URLs visited in web browsers (for content filtering)
- App usage and screen time
- Device location (when enabled by parent)

How Data is Used:
- All monitoring data is analyzed locally on the device using on-device AI
- Only safety alerts (e.g., potential grooming, cyberbullying, inappropriate content) are sent to the parent's device
- No conversation content is transmitted to external servers except encrypted safety summaries
- Data is encrypted and transmitted securely between parent and child devices

Data Storage:
- Monitoring data is stored locally on the child's device
- Parents receive only safety score summaries and alerts
- No data is shared with third parties or used for advertising

Permissions Required:
KOVA requires accessibility service, device admin, notification access, and overlay permissions to function as a parental control tool. These permissions are used solely for child safety monitoring and cannot be used for any other purpose.

Contact: For questions about this privacy policy, contact support.
